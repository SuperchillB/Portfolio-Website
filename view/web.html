allez-cine

First BeCode front-end project. Allez-Cine is a simple movie-rental simulator I built using HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript/jQuery and AJAX/JSON and the MovieDB API to fetch movie data and display it on the website.

dorothycares ai (v.1.0)

DorothyCares is an AI platform built by nine BeCode students and designed to guide fellow BeCoders throughout their training and provide them with administrative, technical and personal assistance. As one of the co-founders of this project, I was in charge of redesigning and developing a new and dynamic front-end structure using vanilla JavaScript, Anime JS and GSAP, HTML5 and the latest CSS3 features. I have also helped to improve the content of the app using Dialogflow.

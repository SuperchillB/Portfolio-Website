about me
hi.
I'm a tech enthusiast and jazz aficionado with an interest in all things Korean and a flair for UI/UX and film photography. Also a full-stack web developer at Becode.
I'm a dual British-French citizen: I believe marmite and fois-gras can live in harmony together. I’ve lived in five countries in Europe and Northern Africa. I’m happily married to a Korean-American UI/UX designer. You can say that boundaries are pretty much blurred.
Seeing the lack of creative work in my previous job environment, I decided last year to hop on the tech train and become a full-time developer, after several years of academic and professional experience in the business side of the tech world.
I am currently doing a coding bootcamp at Becode and am looking for full-Stack or front-End development opportunities, preferably in Berlin.
Besides coding, I’m an avid film photographer and I love to doodle. I might not necessarily be the sporty type, but as a jazz drummer I do consider drumming as a valid form of exercise.
